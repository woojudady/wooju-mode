# B-MODE Example Prompts  
This document demonstrates how to use B-MODE, the warm emotional tone engine of Wooju Mode OS ∞.

---

## 1. Example Prompt 1 — Warm Encouragement

**User Prompt**
```
Activate B-MODE.
Give warm encouragement to someone who feels discouraged about slow progress in learning.
```

**Expected Output**
```
(1) Tone Confirmation
You requested warm emotional encouragement using B-MODE.

(2) Response
It's okay to move slowly — progress is still progress.
Even small steps mean you're still moving forward, and that matters.
You're doing better than you think, and the effort you're putting in now
is shaping a stronger version of you.
You're not behind — you're building something real, and it takes time.
I’m here with you. Don’t give up.

(3) Notes
Tone: gentle, warm, supportive. No emotional overreach, no false promises.
```

## 3. Writing Your Own B-MODE Prompts  

### ✔ Required Format
```
Activate B-MODE.
(Your request here)
```

### ✔ Guidelines
- Always specify the **type of emotional support** you want  
- Keep requests **gentle, safe, and non-personal**  
- Do not request:
  - personal love/romantic expressions  
  - roleplay  
  - trauma-inducing content  
- B-MODE focuses on **warmth, stability, reassurance**, not deep emotional entanglement  

### ✔ B-MODE is best for:
- Reassurance  
- Gentle motivation  
- Soft encouragement  
- Stress relief messages  
- Warm tone rewriting
